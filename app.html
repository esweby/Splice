Welcome to Splice

It wasn't the floods that killed off the humans. It wasn't the wars that finished us off. Our slow demise is coming from a different source. In 2058 a virus started spreading throughout the world that started transforming regular humans into sentient trees with a singular purpose, the destruction of mankind.

Advancements in genetic engineering has given us a fighting chance. You are tasked with creating a new fighting force of new species, combined from the old. This will take place in the arena, against another researcher.
